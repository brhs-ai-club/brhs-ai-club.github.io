About BRHS AI/ML Club

A student-run community for exploring AI and machine learning at BRHS

Our Mission

Curiosity Community Ethics Impact.

The BRHS AI/ML Club is a student-led space to explore machine learning through projects, discussions, and collaboration. We focus on building practical skills while thinking critically about how AI affects people and communities.

Why AI/ML?

AI is shaping nearly every field from medicine to music. In our club, students learn how models are trained, how data drives decisions, and how to build projects that solve real problems. Whether you are brand new or already coding, we help you grow at your own pace.

How to Join

All BRHS students are welcome. No experience required - just bring curiosity.

Meeting Info

Fridays, 2:03-3:00 PM - Room 1002

Who Can Join

Any BRHS student interested in AI, data science, or coding.

What to Bring

A laptop if you have one, and a willingness to experiment.
